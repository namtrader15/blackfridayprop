How do I actually pay for the Challenge? ›

No. Your FTMO account, login, and trading activity remain private between you and FTMO. This page never requests your credentials and cannot view your trades.

In general, FTMO does not allow stacking multiple discounts on top of one another. This KOL voucher is meant to be used as a single, standalone discount. Please refer to FTMO’s official terms for any limitations.
